### About the Experiment 

2-D gel electrophoresis (2DE) is a powerful tool in proteomics that combines two techniques namely Isoelectric focussing (IEF) and Sodium dodecyl sulphate-polyacrylamide gel electrophoresis (SDS-PAGE) to separate and analyze a mixture of complex proteins extracted from biological samples.
